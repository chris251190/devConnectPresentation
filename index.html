What is DevConnect?

DevConnect is an open source platform for people that are interested in IT

Vision

The vision is to connect developers of all genders and ethnicities and make tech more diverse.

It shall offer the possibility for people to work together on exciting community projects.

Values

  • Respect, open-mindedness and tolerance
  • Open communication & listening to others

Focus group(s)

  • Everyone interested in IT
  • People who want to learn new IT skills in project work
  • People who want to teach and share their IT knowledge
  • People who are looking to collaborate on exciting new projects

How does it work?

Possible scenario 1

Lets say Tom wants to learn ReactJS and he is an experienced Java developer. He logs into DevConnect and updates his profile so everybody can know that he could potentially teach Java. And then we have Lisa, she is a React programmer and wants to learn more about Java. They could communicate over DevConnect and find a space where they could meet for exchanging their knowledge.

Possible scenario 2

Jana has no clue about programming but wants to realize an exciting project. Now she logs into DevConnect and creates a project page where she will put the information on what the site is about. She doesn't care about any technical requirements. Now other people can see her project and discuss about it and hopefully Jana will find enough people that will realize this project with her.
